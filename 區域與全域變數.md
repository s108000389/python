- locals()
```
for i in range(1,10):
    locals()['number'+str(i)] = i
    print('Print In Once: ', locals()['number'+str(i)])
```

- globals()
```
for i in range(1,10):
    globals()['number'+str(i)] = i
    print('Print In Once: ', globals()['number'+str(i)])
```
